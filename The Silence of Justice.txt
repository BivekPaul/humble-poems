The Silence of Justice
**********************

Once there was a girl in Hathras,
Joyful, lively, smiling at us.
She dwelt in a hut, forgotten by all,
Yet with hopes and dreams to follow.

On that day, she was hapless,
For beasts awaited, filled with lust.
No one around, to answer her call,
They harassed and invaded her womb.
